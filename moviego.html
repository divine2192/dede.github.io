Movie Go, 2022

Role: Creative direction, design direction
Deliverables: Branding, User Scenerias, Wireframes, Screen Mockup

From user research, most people agree that going to the cinema is always about the movie that they want to watch and less about snacks or waiting in the queue at the cashier to get snack, it is a waste time. Going to the cinema is a place of safety, adventure and learning for many.

The movie experience is what users really care about. It is also a form of self care, the screen is bigger and clearer at the cinema. The sound is louder and sharper. With the lockdown and people finally able to visit the cinema, they need more drive to leave the comfort of their house and visit a cinema.

Driven by my interest, I wanted to contribute to cinema and the experience of moviegoers in this time after the pandemic.








User Experience is understanding what excites users and cater to their needs. The goal of the project was to create a movie dedicated app that helps users to find and discover great movies to watch and nearby cinemas whilst giving them something special to enhance their experience at the cinema which they cannot get when sitting at home.

The app allows users to book tickets and pre-book snacks for the cinema of their choice. Users are able to input their location details using a location tracker and the app finds a list of the nearest cinemas for users can to book their movie day out alone or with friends and family.
