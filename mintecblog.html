Mintec Insight Page, 2019

Role: Creative direction, design direction
Deliverables: Wireframes, Sketch Mockup, Mobile Responsive Design

With the new and drive to improve the marketing strategy for the business for promotion online and keep its customer updated about research and analytical findings. Business blogging is a marketing tactic that uses blogging to get business online visibility. It can help support business growth and client visit on the website.

Working as the designer for the marketing team at Mintec and interested in developing my UX skills, I was tasked to design the blog section to go on the website. My goal was to create a simple yet informative landing page which is easy for clients to navigate, search for interested articles on commodities and also engage and keep them on the page.








Our Insight for mintecglobal is a blog page on the company’s website which shows market movers within the industry, it highlights summary of key movements and news for commodities such as commodity prices, price charts, data and news. I did this by Using a header banner to introduce the reader to the page, a search bar so its easy to search for articles once they visit the page.

Filter by topic by the right makes it easier to select and articles change on the page relating to the topics When user clicks an article they easily ready the article, also tells them how long it will take to read the article at the top with suggestions of articles they might like. It has easy navigation - homepage - article. At the end, successfully developed the webpage with the development team in china and was able to send them design reviews and the page was successfully launched by mintecglobal. www.mintecglobal.com/insight
