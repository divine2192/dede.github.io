Metronet partnership pitch, 2018

Role: Creative direction, design direction
Deliverables: Microsoft Powerpoint Presentation Deck

Metronet as a internet provider in need to partner with other organisation wanted to create presentation pits town new and potential clients by highlighting their key selling strategy and why they are number 1 in the market.They needed a compiling presentation deck that is visual and easy to win clients during a presentation. Metronet is the UK’s most advanced hybrid wired and wireless Internet Service Provider.

I was tasked to design a powerpoint presentation which can be easily used and animated slides. I did this by creating the design in InDesign and then moving it to powerpoint for the final design. i used the company brand such as logo , icons and fonts, created simple and sophisticated animation that were easily. using big fonts and less images for each screen will allow the viewers to focus on what the presenter is saying, the big ext makes it easy for the viewer to read quickly and from a distance.








The icon makes it easy to associate and recognise what the slide page is about quickly. The colour is consistent and branded to make the slide deck coherent and adds a professional tone of voice to the presentation. I was appraised for this project by the marketing manager i was working with and was told that the client was very happy with the design and got a lot of customer interest.
