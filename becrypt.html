Becrypt, 2018

Role: Designer
Deliverables: PDF Infographic

Stealing intellectual property for profit or power is not a new phenomenon. In the digital era, IP is a major target for cybercriminals. Enterprising cyber thieves and criminal gangs can sell corporate IP such as patented innovations or trade secrets to company competitors. Protecting data has never been more critical. IP theft is the number 1 crime in the UK.

Becrypt provides endpoint protection products and services that help organisations focus on their core business activities. Using government-accredited security platforms and standards, they help customers safely and rapidly deploy mobile and end-user devices to access cloud services, driving greater business flexibility, agility and productivity. I was tasked to design a highly visual infographic to help educate customers about protecting their business form IP theft.








I did this by sourcing iconography relating to the content produced Created metaphor and easily understood graphic representing the data Created a logical hierarchy in the data to tell the story about the threats to data loss and steps that can be taken in 5 simple steps

- [ ] Made the Narrative Engaging by simplifying it
- [ ] Present Complex Information In Simple Words And Visuals using iconography and simply words such as figures and percentages
- [ ] Basic Design Structure. reding from top to bottom
- [ ] Creative Touch To Get the reader Focus. This was sucessful and increased customer awareness of cyberthreat and increase the campaign for becrypt.
